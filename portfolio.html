Fitchen YouTube Channel

Fitchen Team Members
  • We produce two-minute healthy eating short videos to share creative healthy dish recipes, with a focus on food minorities such as vegetarians, athletes and other dieters with individual needs.
  • This channel was made for the sole purpose of helping people to have a healthy diet by providing a variety of recipes about how to cook nutritious and delicious food at the same time in a simple way.
  • We have five people in our team. I'm the co-producer taking charge of video production and social media platforms. I write all introductions and recipes!
  • Our cookbook is on the way! Stay tuned :)
